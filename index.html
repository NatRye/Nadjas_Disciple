Mismanaged Plastic Waste Per Capita

This bar chart visualizes the extent of plastic pollution on a per capita basis across different countries. The height of each bar represents the amount of mismanaged plastic waste, measured in kilograms, for each country's population. A higher bar indicates a higher level of plastic waste generated per person. The chart aims to highlight variations in the mismanagement of plastic waste, emphasizing the need for sustainable waste management practices to mitigate the environmental impact of plastic pollution. Explore the data to gain insights into the countries that face significant challenges in handling plastic waste on a per capita basis.
